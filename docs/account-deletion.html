Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your TimeLens account and all associated data, you can request deletion by clicking the button below.

Request Account Deletion
What happens when you request deletion:
  • We will verify your identity using the email address associated with your account
  • Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

What data will be deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile picture from Google Sign-In
  • Purchase history: Records of any in-app purchases (note: you can still restore purchases if you reinstall)
  • App preferences: Any settings or preferences stored in the app
Important: Photos you select in the app are processed in real-time and are never stored on our servers. They are sent to Google's Gemini API for transformation and immediately discarded after processing.
